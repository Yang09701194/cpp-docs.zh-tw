# 具有二元運算子的運算式
[!INCLUDE[vs2017banner](../assembler/inline/includes/vs2017banner.md)]

二元運算子會在運算式中的兩個運算元上作用。  二元運算子為：  
  
-   [乘法類運算子](../cpp/multiplicative-operators-and-the-modulus-operator.md)  
  
    -   乘法 \(\*\)  
  
    -   除法 \(\/\)  
  
    -   模數 \(%\)  
  
-   [加法類運算子](../cpp/additive-operators-plus-and.md)  
  
    -   加法 \(\+\)  
  
    -   減法 \(–\)  
  
-   [移位運算子](../cpp/left-shift-and-right-shift-operators-input-and-output.md)  
  
    -   右移位 \(\>\>\)  
  
    -   左移位 \(\<\<\)  
  
-   [關係與相等運算子](../cpp/relational-operators-equal-and-equal.md)  
  
    -   小於 \(\<\)  
  
    -   大於 \(\>\)  
  
    -   小於或等於 \(\<\=\)  
  
    -   大於或等於 \(\>\=\)  
  
    -   等於 \(\=\=\)  
  
    -   不等於 \(\!\=\)  
  
-   位元運算子  
  
    -   [位元 AND \(&\)](../cpp/bitwise-and-operator-amp.md)  
  
    -   [位元互斥 OR \(^\)](../cpp/bitwise-exclusive-or-operator-hat.md)  
  
    -   [位元包含 OR \(&#124;\)](../cpp/bitwise-inclusive-or-operator-pipe.md)  
  
-   邏輯運算子  
  
    -   [邏輯 AND \(&&\)](../cpp/logical-and-operator-amp-amp.md)  
  
    -   [邏輯 OR \(&#124;&#124;\)](../cpp/logical-or-operator-pipe-pipe.md)  
  
-   [指派運算子](../cpp/assignment-operators.md)  
  
    -   指派 \(\=\)  
  
    -   加法指派 \(\+\=\)  
  
    -   減法指派 \(–\=\)  
  
    -   乘法指派 \(\*\=\)  
  
    -   除法指派 \(\/\=\)  
  
    -   模數指派 \(%\=\)  
  
    -   左移位指派 \(\<\<\=\)  
  
    -   右移位指派 \(\>\>\=\)  
  
    -   位元 AND 指派 \(&\=\)  
  
    -   位元互斥 OR 指派 \(^\=\)  
  
    -   位元包含 OR 指派 \(&#124;\=\)  
  
-   [逗號運算子](../cpp/comma-operator.md) \(,\)
